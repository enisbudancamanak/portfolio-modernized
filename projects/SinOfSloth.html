Sin Of Sloth

Game Dev
HTML, CSS & JavaScript
Phaser 3

In a team of five, where I was the lead developer, we developed "Sin Of Sloth". The pixel art 2D platform game deals with the everyday fears and stress in the life of a student who finds himself in an endless nightmare. The level environment combines everyday life and absurd fantasies as the main character tries to escape the pursuit of a giant hand. The deliberate choice of an endless level structure and teleporters emphasizes the inability to distinguish reality from fantasy, while collected items represent everyday objects.
