ARthlete

App Dev
Augmented Reality
Unity
Image Tracking

In a creative team with three other students, we created the fascinating project "ARthlete". This innovative augmented reality application offers a unique way to explore fitness exercises. From any perspective, users can immerse themselves in the world of the demonstrated exercises and experience them interactively. ARthlete takes the fitness experience to a new level, allowing users to view movements from multiple angles. This project combines technology and fitness in a compelling way, creating an immersive environment to give users a unique experience. Immerse yourself in the world of ARthlete and experience fitness in a new dimension.
