Slime Hero

Game Dev
Phaser 3
JavaScript

Slime Hero is an addictive 2D Metroidvania, Action-RPG-Adventure web game developed by a creative team of three dedicated students. Players take control of "Slime" and journey through a fantastical and mysterious world. Along the way, they will be accompanied by an enchanting fairy-like creature named Fairy. Together, they set out to unravel the mysteries of unknown lands and find answers to questions about their identity and their mysterious stay in this world. Immerse yourself in the charming atmosphere of Slime Hero and experience an exciting adventure full of action, puzzles and discoveries.

Play it yourself!
