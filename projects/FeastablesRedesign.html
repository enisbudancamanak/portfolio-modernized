Feastables Redesign

UI Design
Web Dev
E-Commerce

Dissatisfied with the original Feastables website, I started a journey to redesign the site and capture the unique flair of the chocolate brand. My main focus was to integrate micro-animations to create a more interactive and engaging online experience. This redesign aimed to take the brand identity to a new level through visual elements and an improved user experience. Immerse yourself in the redesigned Feastables website and experience the fusion of aesthetic design and engaging animation.
