Wagenfeld Redesign

Web Dev

In a collaborative project with two other students, we have created an impressive minimalistic product. Our goal was to develop a redesign in cooperation with the Wagenfeld Haus in Bremen that would give the exhibition a special, modern touch. By combining design aesthetics and functionality, we have created a contemporary and appealing reinterpretation that highlights the unique characteristics of the Wagenfeld Haus. Immerse yourself in this innovative redesign and experience how minimalist design blurs the boundaries between aesthetics and functionality in a captivating way.
