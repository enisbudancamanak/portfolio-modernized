Interactive Graphics

Data Visualizing
HTML, CSS & JavaScript
D3.js

In this project, I developed a website that presents interactive data graphs on the university statistics of my degree program over the past few years. My focus was to create a user-friendly experience that allows visitors to easily navigate between different time periods and sections and gain detailed insights. The modern "glass morphism" look and dashboard design is intended to optimize the user experience and present the data in an engaging and easy to understand way.
