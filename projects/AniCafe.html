Together with two other students, I ventured into a comprehensive "redesign" of the app MyAnimeList, an anime tracker. Our goal was to create a product that would not only make it easier to discover new anime, but also allow users to add them to a personalized list and edit them according to their preferences. This creative collaboration resulted in an innovative application that not only improves functionality, but also takes the anime enthusiast experience to a new level. Immerse yourself in our visionary redesign of the MyAnimeList app and experience a more intuitive and engaging way to organize your anime world.
